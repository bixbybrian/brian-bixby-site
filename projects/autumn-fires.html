Autumn Fires

Autumn Fires, acrylic and oil on canvas, 2018.

Project Overview

These three paintings were made during a residency at the Iconotop Gallery in Weimar, Germany. Using a combination of oil and acrylic paints I built up and broke down the surface of the paintings during the 3-month residency. Bright colors and complex naturalistic patterns emerge from each canvas. The fine details of this Triptych pull you closer to inspect and discover the mysteries hidden beneath the surface.
