In The Darkest Places

In The Darkest Places (Gerbera), digital, 6000 × 9000 pixels.

Project Overview

This work is composed of a mixture of disparate elements: photographs of flowers, pornography, and fashion advertising. What we see is not always what we imagine it to be — especially true in an era of digital media which allows history to be re-written and re-created and the truth is no longer an absolute.
