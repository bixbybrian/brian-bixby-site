Memory of a Desert Journey

Memory of a Desert Journey, installation view, 2-channel video projection, GlogauAir, Berlin, Germany, 2011.

Project Overview

Memory of a Desert Journey started with a collage of high resolution digital photos taken in the New Mexico desert during the summer of 2010. The final image was composed in Photoshop as a very long horizon that begins the same as it ends — creating a perfect loop. This composition was then animated and additional digital animation was layered on top of the scrolling image.

Midori Hirano created sound design elements inspired by the desert photographs and mixed these together with several field recordings she made in San Francisco and on the island of Crete, Greece.

Collaboration with Jeffers Egan and Midori Hirano.
